# Local Discovery Data Sources

The **Shops Near You** feature in Desi Digest utilizes a hybrid data approach to provide a realistic local discovery experience without making false partnership claims or violating data scraping rules.

## 1. Public Search Seeds
These entries represent widely known, public wholesale and retail markets in Dhaka (e.g., Karwan Bazar, Mohammadpur Krishi Market). 
- **Goal:** Provide starting points for finding fresh, whole ingredients.
- **Source:** General public knowledge and generic Google Maps searches.
- **Status:** Not affiliated with Desi Digest.

## 2. Google Maps Search Helpers
These entries act as dynamic search triggers (e.g., "Fresh Vegetables near me").
- **Goal:** Leverage the user's browser geolocation (if permitted) to open a relevant Google Maps search query.
- **Source:** Procedural map queries based on food categories.

## 3. Curated Demo / Sample Partners
These are fictional entries created purely to demonstrate how official partnerships or home-kitchen listings might look in a future marketplace iteration.
- **Goal:** UI/UX demonstration.
- **Status:** Clearly labeled with "Sample Partner" and "Demo data" badges. 

## Trust & Safety Disclaimer
Desi Digest **does not** act as an ecommerce checkout platform. We do not scrape live prices or live availability from competitors like Foodpanda or Chaldal. Users are always advised to call or check the shop/market directly before visiting. General nutrition guidance only — not medical advice.
