## Detailed Contributions

### Tasfiq Tasin (Technical Lead & Full-Stack AI Engineer)
Tasfiq drove the core architecture, backend systems, and the complex AI/database integrations that make Desi Digest smart and reliable. His contributions include:
- **Backend & Database:** Architected the Supabase setup, including Auth, Database schemas, Storage integrations, and complex RLS policies.
- **AI & Integrations:** Built the Gemini AI model integration, handling everything from food knowledge retrieval to embeddings and RAG setups.
- **Plate Analyzer:** Developed the photo-based meal analysis feature, ensuring accurate nutrition estimations from images.
- **Smart Health Nudge System:** Built the highly personalized, culturally aware Smart Health Nudge system. This includes the robust, offline-safe deterministic fallbacks, the curated local image mapping strategy (ensuring real, licensed photos always appear), multi-language support (Bangla/English), and the Plan 3 Verification for strict AI safety.
- **Local Discovery & Bridges:** Developed the "Shops Near You" local discovery bridge and the "Raw Food Basket" feature. This involved setting up privacy-first, zero-checkout integrations with Google Maps using browser geolocation and safe external search strategies.
- **Meal & Nutrition Parsing:** Created the sophisticated manual Banglish meal nutrition parser, ensuring that typed meals (like "gorur mangsho dim vat") correctly estimate macros without relying on external APIs.
- **Deployment & QA:** Handled Vercel/Supabase production fixes, deployment debugging, model routing, schema fallback handling, and ensured zero regressions across typechecks and builds. Managed the repository's overall final submission readiness.
